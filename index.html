Parts of Speech

The jobs words do in Sentences

PART MAIN JOB EXAMPLES
Nouns Name of a person, place , thing or Idea Lady, Road, Tree, Love, dream, Pencil, Bob
Pronouns Takes the Place of a noun He, She, If, You, Were
Verbs Expresses action or a state of being Ran, Flew, trips, was, is, showed, jumps
Adjectives Describes a Noun New, Shiny, Fat, Thorough, Funny
Adverbs Describes or Modifies a Verb, Adjective or another Adverb Quickly, Repeatedly, Precisely (often end in -ly)
Prepositions Establish a relationship between the noun and the rest of the sentence Over, Around, Through, During, According, To, Above, Beside
conjuctions Connects words, phrases and Clauses And, But, Or, Nor, However, Morever
Interjections Express surprise or other emotions Ouch!, Eek!, Ow!, Yikes!, Hooray !, (often has an exclamation point )
Articles Help defince nouns The, An, A
